Our Story
Rooted in Ashanti Region, Growing for Ghana

From the golden grain fields of Ejisu to the lush avocado slopes of Mampong, Chibsah Farms breathes life into the rich soils of the Ashanti Region. Established in 2024 by YAC Group, our story is woven into the fabric of Asanteman - where the cocoa-colored earth meets the ambition of a new agricultural generation.

Our Guiding Principles

Vision

"To be West Africa's beacon of sustainable agro-innovation by 2030"
We envision thriving rural communities where smart farming and ancestral wisdom unite to create food security for generations

Motto

Eat Healthy! Grow Healthy!! Live Healthy!!

Mission

Nurturing Land, Feeding Nations

    Revolutionize traditional farming through climate-smart techniques
    Empower 100+ local farming cooperatives annually
    Deliver premium organic products from our fields to your table
    Preserve Ashanti's agricultural heritage through youth training programs

Strategic Aims

    Land Stewardship: Cultivate 5,000+ acres using regenerative practices by 2025
    Community Impact: Create 1,200 direct farm jobs across Ashanti Region
    Environmental Pledge: Achieve 100% renewable energy use in poultry operations by 2026
    Market Leadership: Become Ghana's #1 supplier of organic Hass avocados

Our Various Farms

Livestock Farm

Our free-range cattle, goats, and sheep graze on pesticide-free pastures across Ejura and Mampong. Implementing the Akan principle of "Ahotor" (wholesome care), we combine modern veterinary science with ancestral animal welfare practices.
