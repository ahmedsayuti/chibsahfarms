Our Story

Founded in 2020, Chibsah Farms began with a simple vision: to provide Ghana with premium organic agricultural products while practicing sustainable farming that respects our environment and supports our local communities.

What started as a small family operation in the Ashanti Region has grown into one of Ghana's leading sustainable farms, specializing in organic livestock, poultry, avocados, and ancient grains.

Inspired by our Chairman, Yussif Chibsah, we have chosen a name that embodies our mission: to be a blessing. We strive to live up to this name by being a blessing to our community, our customers, and our environment through our ethical farming

Sustainable Practices

At Chibsah Farms, we believe that sustainable agriculture is the foundation of food security and environmental health. Our farming methods are designed to work in harmony with nature.

We employ rotational grazing techniques that prevent soil degradation, use solar-powered irrigation systems, and maintain biodiversity corridors to protect local ecosystems.

All our products are certified organic by the Ghana Organic Agriculture Network, ensuring they're free from synthetic pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers.

Community Impact

Chibsah Farms is deeply committed to the communities we operate in. We believe that agricultural development should benefit both people and the planet.

We provide training programs for local farmers on sustainable practices, offer educational farm tours for schools, and donate a portion of our produce to local food banks and community initiatives.

Through our "Farm to Future" program, we sponsor agricultural education for promising students from rural communities, helping to build the next generation of sustainable farmers in Ghana.
