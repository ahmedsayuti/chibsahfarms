About Chibsah Farms

❮ ❯

From the golden grain fields of Ejisu to the lush avocado slopes of Mampong, Chibsah Farms breathes life into the rich soils of the Ashanti Region. Established in 2024 by YAC Group, our story is woven into the fabric of Asanteman - where the cocoa-colored earth meets the ambition of a new agricultural generation.

Across 12 farming communities spanning from Bekwai to Offinso, we honor Ashanti's farming legacy while writing new chapters in sustainable agriculture. Our grain silos in Ejura stand beside ancient kente weaving villages, blending tradition with modern food security solutions. The clucking rhythms of our Ahinsan poultry farms harmonize with the morning chants of local gong-gong beaters.

In the shadow of Lake Bosomtwe, our avocado orchards thrive under the same sun that once guided Ashanti kings. Near Bonwire's famous kente looms, our livestock graze on open pastures, their health mirroring our commitment to Ahotor - the Akan principle of wholesome sustenance.

At Chibsah Farms, every harvest is a homecoming - to our land, our people, and our sacred duty to feed Ghana's future.
