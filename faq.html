What sustainable practices do you use in livestock rearing?

Our sustainable practices include:

  • Rotational grazing across 5,000 acres to prevent overgrazing
  • Solar-powered water systems for reduced carbon footprint
  • Organic waste recycling program that converts waste to compost
  • Biodiversity conservation zones to protect local ecosystems
  • Natural pest control methods to minimize chemical use

Are your farming methods completely organic?

Yes, we are proud to be certified organic by Ghana Organic Agriculture Network. Our practices include:

  • No synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers
  • Non-GMO feed for all livestock
  • Natural soil enrichment through composting and crop rotation
  • Ethical treatment of all animals with access to open pastures

We undergo annual inspections to maintain our organic certification status.

What makes your eggs different from regular eggs?

Our Omega-3 eggs come from free-range hens that enjoy:

  • Access to open pastures with diverse vegetation
  • Organic feed supplemented with flaxseed for higher Omega-3 content
  • No antibiotics or growth hormones
  • Natural lighting and ventilation in their coops

Nutritional benefits include:

  • 25% less saturated fat than conventional eggs
  • 40% more Vitamin A
  • Twice the Omega-3 fatty acids
  • Rich in Vitamin E and beta-carotene

How does your farm contribute to environmental conservation?

Environmental stewardship is at the core of our operations:

  • Water Conservation: We use drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting systems
  • Soil Health: Crop rotation and cover cropping maintain soil fertility
  • Carbon Sequestration: Our pastures capture significant amounts of carbon dioxide
  • Wildlife Protection: We maintain 100 acres of natural habitat for native species
  • Renewable Energy: 60% of our energy needs are met through solar power

We're proud to have received the Ghana Environmental Protection Agency's Green Award for three consecutive years.

What are your delivery options and timelines?

We offer several delivery options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days within Ghana
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days to major cities
  • International Shipping: Available for selected products to neighboring countries

All orders are packed in eco-friendly, insulated packaging with ice packs to ensure freshness. We monitor temperatures during transit to guarantee quality.

Orders placed before 12 PM are processed the same day. Friday orders for delivery to remote areas are processed the following Monday.
